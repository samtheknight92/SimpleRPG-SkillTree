# Career Identity Guide

Each career has **four defining traits**.

These traits are **design pillars**, not flavour text.

- Every career skill should reinforce **at least one** of these traits.
- **Career fusion** skills should reinforce at least one trait from **both** parent careers.
- Do **not** write skills that contradict these identities unless deliberately designing a rare capstone.

Career tree keys in data match the headings below (e.g. `blacksmith`, display name **Blacksmith**).

**Careers vs combat styles:** Weapon and magic trees define *how* a character fights. Careers define *what job* they bring to the party — crafting at camp, healing between fights, formation fighting, investigation, and so on. A Soldier may use any weapon; Soldier traits are about **team defence and focus fire**, not sword technique.

**Harmony:** Where noted under a career, existing `+ Harmony Reaction:` / `+ Harmony:` lines in skill text stay as written. Traits explain *why* those harmonies fit; they do not change the numbers.

## 🔨 Blacksmith (`blacksmith`)

| Trait | Meaning |
|-------|---------|
| **Armour & Armsmithing** | Forge weapons, armour, and metal kit that allies equip. Skills add craft recipes and make the party materially better prepared for the next fight. |
| **Masterwork Tempering** | Improve what you make — harder hits, sturdier protection, or finer alloys on gear **you** forged. Rewards putting smithing effort into specific items rather than generic bonuses. |
| **Battlefield Fitting** | Tune ally gear before battle so they enter the fight sharper. Pre-combat preparation, not mid-swing repairs. |
| **Lockbreaking & Breach** | Break mundane locks, hinges, chains, and obstacles with tools — siege and camp problems, not a second weapon tree. |

*Harmony:* **Battlefield Fitting** — multiple Smiths tuning the **same ally** grant both prep bonuses (see Field Fit).

## 🍳 Chef (`chef`)

| Trait | Meaning |
|-------|---------|
| **Hearty Provisions** | Cook meals that restore HP and keep the party fed on the road. The baseline “camp cook” fantasy — food as survival. |
| **Battlefield Provisions** | Prepare food that buffs allies **before or during** a fight — Stamina, Accuracy, initiative, and similar short-lived combat edges. |
| **Camp Provisioning** | Stretch ingredients and time: extra portions, longer feast duration, travel rations. Makes the Chef valuable between combats without new subsystems. |
| **Spoil & Poison Sense** | Detect spoiled, drugged, or poisoned food by taste or smell before anyone eats. Protects the table from bad meals and suspicious feasts. |

*Harmony:* **Battlefield Provisions** and **Camp Provisioning** — Banquet Planner and Battle Breakfast; count Chefs helping on the **same feast or meal**.

## 💊 Medic (`medic`)

| Trait | Meaning |
|-------|---------|
| **Wound Mending** | Brew potions, apply bandages, and restore HP through crafted and direct care. The core healer loop at camp and in a pinch. |
| **Triage & Diagnosis** | Assess what is wrong — bleeding, poison, disease, HP band — before choosing treatment. Information first, then mending. |
| **Plague & Poison Warding** | Protect the party from ailments spreading or landing: antidote training, cleansing, and auras that bolster saves vs disease and poison. |
| **Stabilise & Revive** | Pull allies back from the brink — stabilize at 0 HP, clear minor debuffs, rare revival drafts. “They were down; now they can act again.” |

*Harmony:* **Plague & Poison Warding** — Plague Ward; +1 Magical Defence vs disease and poison per Medic with the aura in range (no Reaction).

---

## ⚗️ Alchemist (`alchemist`)

| Trait | Meaning |
|-------|---------|
| **Volatile Brews** | Craft thrown acids, bombs, and reactive mixtures that deal damage and apply debuffs in combat. |
| **Smoke & Distraction** | Deploy smoke, flash powder, and similar tricks to confuse foes or cover retreats — utility without full spellcasting. |
| **Reagent Identification** | Safely identify unknown liquids and powders — poison, potion, or inert — before anyone drinks or throws them. |
| **Material Transmutation** | Convert common materials into alchemical bases and supplies. Camp economy and crafting depth, not a combat nova button. |

*Harmony:* **Volatile Brews** — Acid Vials; each Alchemist joining on the **same target, same round** adds +1 acid damage on hit.

---

## ✨ Enchanter (`enchanter`)

| Trait | Meaning |
|-------|---------|
| **Rune Inscription** | Apply enchants and typed bonuses to weapons and armour — stat chips, elemental ink, minor permanent effects. |
| **Protective Warding** | Inscribe wards and charms that raise Magical Defence or suppress hostile magic on gear and bearers. |
| **Arcane Appraisal** | Inspect a magic item and learn its properties, curse risk, and safe use — before anyone attunes or equips it. |
| **Master Engraving** | High-end work: extra enchantment slots, soul binding, owner locks. Rare items that feel **made**, not looted generic. |

*Harmony:* Same-career harmony deferred until co-enchant flow exists; traits still guide skill writing.

## 🔍 Detective (`detective`)

*(Recipe lineage from old “archaeology” professions maps here — ancient sites and relic lore live under **Trail & Timeline Reconstruction** and **Clue Synthesis**.)*

| Trait | Meaning |
|-------|---------|
| **Scene Examination** | Study a place or object and surface clues — obvious, hidden, or secret tiers. The “what happened here?” opener. |
| **Witness Interrogation** | Question people, read lies, and pressure answers in social scenes. People are evidence too. |
| **Trail & Timeline Reconstruction** | Follow tracks, rebuild recent events, and determine where someone went. Wilderness trails and ruined halls both apply. |
| **Clue Synthesis** | Connect clues the party already knows into a inference the GM must treat as useful. Rewards note-taking, not a new roll every time. |

*Harmony:* **Scene Examination** — Keen Eye; each Detective joining on the **same scene** adds +1 clue tier.

## 🌲 Ranger (`ranger`)

| Trait | Meaning |
|-------|---------|
| **Wilderness Tracking** | Follow tracks, read the land, and keep the party moving through wild country — who passed, how many, which way. |
| **Snares & Ambush** | Set snares and alarms, hide camps, and gain initiative when scouts have time to prepare. Fight on prepared ground. |
| **Wilderness Vigilance** | Spot ambushes, hidden foes, and traps before they trigger — for you or allies on overwatch. |
| **Marked Quarry** | Call out a target so **allies** gain accuracy on their next shot or strike. Wilderness coordinator, not a solo archer tree. |

*Harmony:* **Marked Quarry** — Volley Call; each Ranger joining marks one more ally and adds +1 to marked allies’ next attack vs that target.

---

## 🎯 Marksman (`marksman`)

| Trait | Meaning |
|-------|---------|
| **Steady Aim** | Win firefights through accuracy — bonuses to hit, called shots, and ignoring half-cover when the skill says so. |
| **Overwatch Shots** | Hold a ready shot and interrupt enemy movement in your line of sight. Control space by **threat**, not melee reach. |
| **Pinning Shots** | Fire into a zone so foes fight at a penalty — less accurate attacks or hesitant movement for a short time. |
| **Marked for Allies** | Damage or suppress a foe so teammates gain +1 Accuracy against it this round. Setup from the back rank. |

*Harmony:* **Pinning Shots** — Suppressing Fire; each Marksman joining on the **same zone** adds −1 to enemy attacks or movement.

*Distinct from Ranger:* Marksman is **personal ranged mastery and zone control**; Ranger is **wilderness, traps, and calling targets for the group**.

## 🛡️ Soldier (`soldier`)

| Trait | Meaning |
|-------|---------|
| **Shield Formation** | Stand with allies and raise their Physical Defence — adjacent cover, shield wall, holding the line together. |
| **Battlefield Rally** | Shout allies back into the fight — reroll failed saves, restore HP, bump initiative. Morale as mechanics. |
| **Armoured Discipline** | Train and fight in medium or heavy armour; steady +Physical Defence when comrades are near. |
| **Volley Focus** | Reward the squad attacking the **same** enemy — accuracy and bonus damage when focus fire aligns. |

*Harmony:* **Shield Formation** — Shield Wall huddle stacking; **Volley Focus** — Phalanx passive when multiple Soldiers focus the same target.

---

## ⚔️ Paladin (`paladin`)

| Trait | Meaning |
|-------|---------|
| **Oathbound Resolve** | Hold to a sacred vow — stronger saves vs fear and charm, steady Magical Defence. The knight who does not break. |
| **Lay on Hands** | Touch an ally and restore HP directly. Small, reliable healing without a full Medic tree. |
| **Holy Protection** | Shelter allies and yourself — bulwark when wounded, sacred stance, auras that guard nearby companions. |
| **Rebuke the Unholy** | Turn fear back on enemies and ward against undead and demons — judgement on corrupt foes, not generic smiting. |

*Harmony:* **Holy Protection** — Aura of Protection; +1 Magical Defence vs fear and charm per Paladin with the aura in range (no Reaction).

---

## 😤 Berserker (`berserker`)

| Trait | Meaning |
|-------|---------|
| **Wounded Fury** | Fight harder when hurt — bonus damage below half HP, short Rage windows, stamina on kills. Pain fuels the swing. |
| **Reckless Assault** | Trade defence for offence on deliberate all-in attacks. The Berserker chooses the exchange. |
| **Battlefield Intimidation** | Roar, glare, and presence that shrink enemy accuracy in an area — fear without casting spells. |
| **Coup de Grace** | Finish bloodied foes — extra damage against targets already half-dead. Execution, not random crit fishing. |

*Harmony:* **Battlefield Intimidation** — Intimidate; each Berserker joining in the **same area** adds −1 accuracy on failed saves.

---

## 🤺 Duelist (`duelist`)

| Trait | Meaning |
|-------|---------|
| **Dueling Footwork** | Speed and clean exits from melee — disengage from chosen foes, stay mobile with a one-handed blade. |
| **Parry & Riposte** | Negate a blow and answer it — contest parries, strip reactions, punish sloppy attacks. |
| **Feint & Misdirection** | Deceive a single opponent — roll twice and keep the higher attack, force bad reads. Mind game, not area debuffs. |
| **Single-Foe Focus** | Bonuses tied to **one chosen opponent** or foes who have not yet acted — the duel, not the mob fight. |

*Harmony:* Intentionally **solo** — traits express one-on-one outplay; same-career stacking is not planned (cross-career feints may link to Thief or Ranger later).

---

## 🗡️ Thief (`thief`)

| Trait | Meaning |
|-------|---------|
| **Pickpocket & Filch** | Lift small items, swap pouches, and steal unequipped gear in scenes — skulduggery, not battlefield damage. |
| **Slip Away** | Escape grapples, restraints, traps, and melee — disengage, hit-and-run movement, contested breaks. |
| **Shadow Stealth** | Move unnoticed in light armour or none — hide, blend, approach without announcing. |
| **Distraction Setup** | Distract a foe so an **ally’s** next attack is stronger. The Thief enables kills they may never claim. |

*Harmony:* **Distraction Setup** — Dirty Trick; each Thief joining on the **same foe** adds +1 to that ally’s attack.

---

## 📘 Mage — career (`mage`)

*Not the magic element tree. Career Mage skills **support other casters**; fire/ice/lightning trees are separate.*

| Trait | Meaning |
|-------|---------|
| **Spell Empowerment** | Boost an ally’s **next** spell or magical attack — extra damage dice or stronger heals on that one cast. |
| **Circle Warding** | Drop short-lived wards and cleansing assists — Magical Defence bubbles, dispel help on an ally’s next attempt. |
| **Arcane Sustenance** | Help allies keep casting — Stamina when nearby friends cast small spells, shared concentration when the skill allows. |
| **Amplified Aid** | Passive polish on buffs and heals **you** apply to others — slightly stronger outcomes from support actions. |

*Harmony:* **Spell Empowerment** — Empower Ally upgrades when multiple Mages join the **same ally**; **Circle Warding** — Ward Circle huddle stacking.

---

## 🎵 Musician (`musician`)

| Trait | Meaning |
|-------|---------|
| **Sustained Performance** | Songs that last several turns after one Stamina cost — maintain the piece, do not fire-and-forget. |
| **Inspiring Morale** | Raise ally stats within hearing — Strength, Speed, Accuracy, Magical Defence, Stamina ticks as the song describes. |
| **Same-Song Ensemble** | Multiple performers join the **same song ID** via Harmony Reaction; count performers, add the listed bonus. Core Musician identity. |
| **Allied Ballads & Dissonance** | Buff companions (anthems, hymns) or rattle enemies (dissonant notes, accuracy penalties on foes). |

*Harmony:* **Same-Song Ensemble** on Work Song, Marching Tune, Soothing Hymn, Battle Anthem, Dissonant Note — joiners skip their next turn per table Harmony rules.

*Performance note:* Singing works without gear; off-hand **instruments** amplify when that item system ships (see ROADMAP).

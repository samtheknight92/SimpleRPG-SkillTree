# Weapon & Element Identity Guide

Each weapon type and magic element has **four defining traits**.

These traits are **design pillars**, not just flavour text.

- Every skill should reinforce **at least one** of these traits.
- **Fusion skills** should reinforce at least one trait from **both** parent categories.
- Do **not** create skills that contradict these identities unless intentionally creating a unique legendary ability.

Skill tree keys in data match the headings below (e.g. skill tree `ranged`, display name **Ranged**).

---

## ⚔️ Sword (`sword`)

| Trait | Meaning |
|-------|---------|
| **Versatility** | Balanced offence and defence with very few weaknesses. Sword skills should rarely be the strongest or weakest option, instead rewarding adaptability in almost any combat situation. |
| **Technique** | Rewards precision, timing, footwork and exploiting openings in an opponent's defence rather than relying on brute force. |
| **Counterplay** | Specialises in parries, ripostes, defensive reactions and punishing enemy mistakes after blocking or dodging attacks. |
| **Momentum** | Successful attacks naturally flow into additional strikes, movement or combo attacks, encouraging continuous offensive pressure. |

---

## 🏹 Ranged (`ranged`)

| Trait | Meaning |
|-------|---------|
| **Distance** | Excels at defeating enemies safely from afar before they can retaliate. |
| **Precision** | Rewards accurate shots, targeting weak points and carefully choosing high-value targets. |
| **Mobility** | Maintains offensive pressure while moving, repositioning or avoiding melee combat. |
| **Preparation** | Relies on planning through charged attacks, special ammunition, traps or battlefield positioning before striking. |

---

## 🪓 Axe (`axe`)

| Trait | Meaning |
|-------|---------|
| **Brute Force** | Focuses on overwhelming strength and raw physical damage rather than speed or finesse. |
| **Armour Breaking** | Designed to destroy armour, shields, hardened enemies and defensive structures. |
| **Cleaving** | Wide swings naturally damage multiple nearby enemies, making axes powerful against groups. |
| **Relentlessness** | Sacrifices defence and precision in favour of relentless offensive pressure that overwhelms opponents. |

---

## 🪄 Staff (`staff`)

| Trait | Meaning |
|-------|---------|
| **Arcane Focus** | Acts as the ultimate magical weapon, greatly enhancing spellcasting instead of physical combat. |
| **Mana Mastery** | Improves mana efficiency, regeneration and the ability to sustain long magical battles. |
| **Channeling** | Rewards building magical power over time through preparation or sustained spellcasting. |
| **Magical Utility** | Provides powerful support abilities, magical manipulation and battlefield utility beyond simple damage. |

---

## 🗡️ Dagger (`dagger`)

| Trait | Meaning |
|-------|---------|
| **Speed** | Uses rapid attacks and fast combinations to overwhelm opponents before they can react. |
| **Precision** | Targets vulnerable areas, rewarding critical strikes and exploiting weakened enemies. |
| **Agility** | Excels at flanking, repositioning and hit-and-run combat rather than prolonged fights. |
| **Bleeding** | Inflicts stacking wounds that slowly weaken enemies over time through repeated strikes. |

---

## 🔱 Polearm (`polearm`)

| Trait | Meaning |
|-------|---------|
| **Reach** | Controls enemies from a safer distance, attacking before opponents can close the gap. |
| **Battlefield Control** | Pushes, trips, sweeps or repositions enemies to dictate the flow of combat. |
| **Defensive Positioning** | Rewards maintaining proper spacing and punishes enemies who rush recklessly. |
| **Discipline** | Uses measured attacks, patience and tactical positioning instead of reckless aggression. |

---

## 🔨 Hammer (`hammer`)

| Trait | Meaning |
|-------|---------|
| **Crushing Power** | Delivers devastating single hits capable of staggering even the toughest opponents. |
| **Shockwaves** | Heavy impacts affect nearby enemies or the surrounding terrain through sheer force. |
| **Fortitude** | Encourages slow, resilient combat while resisting interruption and enemy crowd control. |
| **Siege Breaking** | Excels at destroying armour, stone, fortifications, constructs and defensive structures. |

---

## 🔥 Fire (`fire`)

| Trait | Meaning |
|-------|---------|
| **Destruction** | Produces some of the highest direct damage through aggressive offensive magic. |
| **Burning** | Ignites enemies, causing lingering damage that continues after the initial attack. |
| **Chaos** | Creates explosions, spreading flames and unpredictable environmental destruction. |
| **Aggression** | Rewards constant offensive pressure instead of defensive or supportive playstyles. |

---

## ❄️ Ice (`ice`)

| Trait | Meaning |
|-------|---------|
| **Control** | Limits enemy movement through slowing, freezing and immobilising effects. |
| **Defence** | Creates protective barriers and defensive magic that increase survivability. |
| **Precision** | Rewards careful planning and tactical spell placement rather than reckless casting. |
| **Shatter** | Frozen enemies become vulnerable to devastating follow-up attacks from you or your allies. |

---

## ⚡ Lightning (`lightning`)

| Trait | Meaning |
|-------|---------|
| **Speed** | Delivers extremely fast attacks and allows rapid offensive combinations. |
| **Chain Energy** | Electricity naturally jumps between nearby enemies, rewarding grouped targets. |
| **Disruption** | Interrupts spellcasting, stuns enemies and overloads magical abilities. |
| **Momentum** | Rewards continuous attacking by building speed, energy or offensive pressure during combat. |

---

## 🌍 Earth (`earth`)

| Trait | Meaning |
|-------|---------|
| **Protection** | Creates strong defensive barriers and protects both yourself and allies. |
| **Strength** | Enhances physical durability, armour and resistance against incoming attacks. |
| **Terrain Manipulation** | Shapes the battlefield using walls, pillars, spikes and other geological formations. |
| **Endurance** | Excels in long fights through resilience, stability and resistance to forced movement. |

---

## 🌪️ Wind (`wind`)

| Trait | Meaning |
|-------|---------|
| **Mobility** | Provides exceptional movement speed, repositioning and battlefield freedom. |
| **Evasion** | Avoids attacks through agility, dodging and superior movement. |
| **Manipulation** | Pushes, pulls or redirects enemies, allies and projectiles across the battlefield. |
| **Flow** | Rewards continuously moving before, during and after attacks to maintain combat momentum. |

---

## 💧 Water (`water`)

| Trait | Meaning |
|-------|---------|
| **Restoration** | Specialises in healing wounds, restoring health and supporting allies. |
| **Adaptability** | Comfortably shifts between offensive, defensive and supportive roles depending on the situation. |
| **Battlefield Flow** | Manipulates water to reposition enemies, slow movement or control combat spaces. |
| **Pressure** | Highly compressed water becomes capable of piercing armour, barriers and durable enemies. |

---

## 🌑 Darkness (`darkness`)

| Trait | Meaning |
|-------|---------|
| **Corruption** | Weakens enemies through curses, decay and long-lasting debilitating effects. |
| **Fear** | Manipulates enemy morale, reducing confidence and limiting combat effectiveness. |
| **Life Drain** | Steals health, mana or stamina from enemies to empower the caster. |
| **Deception** | Uses shadows, concealment and illusion-like effects to outmanoeuvre opponents. |

---

## ☀️ Light (`light`)

| Trait | Meaning |
|-------|---------|
| **Purification** | Removes poison, curses, corruption and other harmful effects from allies. |
| **Protection** | Creates holy barriers, blessings and powerful defensive magic. |
| **Judgement** | Deals exceptional damage against undead, demons, darkness and corrupted creatures. |
| **Inspiration** | Strengthens allies through buffs, morale and divine empowerment instead of raw offence. |

---

## 🥊 Striker (`striker`)

Unarmed martial arts — both hands empty. Display label **Striker**; also covers legacy `unarmed` skill keys.

| Trait | Meaning |
|-------|---------|
| **Empty Hands** | The body is the weapon. Striker skills require both hands free; unarmed Basic Attack scales through training passives instead of gear. |
| **Combo Flow** | Chains multiple Basic Attacks in one action (flurries, volleys, reversal counters) rather than relying on a single heavy hit. |
| **Internal Arts** | Hardens the body through stance and conditioning — Iron Palm, Iron Body, Open Stance — gaining defence without armour or a shield. |
| **Feints & Control** | Deceives, deflects and restrains: feinted openings, slip parries, joint locks and punishing an opponent after they overcommit. |

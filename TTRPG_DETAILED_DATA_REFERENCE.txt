EXAMPLE 1 - Goblin Scout vs Player:
-----------------------------------
Player: Level 2 Human Fighter, Steel Sword, Chain Mail
- HP: 20, Strength: 2, Accuracy: 1, Physical Defence: 12

Goblin Scout: Level 1
- HP: 12, Accuracy: 1, Physical Defence: 9

Combat:
1. Player attacks: d20 + 1 (Accuracy) + 2 (Sword Basics) = 15 vs 9 = HIT
   Damage: d8 (Steel Sword) + 2 (Strength) = 8 damage
   Goblin HP: 12 - 8 = 4 HP remaining

2. Goblin attacks: d20 + 1 (Accuracy) = 12 vs 12 = HIT
   Damage: d4 (Rusty Dagger) + (-1 Strength) = 2 damage
   Player HP: 20 - 2 = 18 HP remaining

3. Player attacks: d20 + 1 + 2 = 18 vs 9 = HIT
   Damage: d8 + 2 = 7 damage
   Goblin HP: 4 - 7 = -3 = DEFEATED

Loot: 5 Lumens, Rusty Dagger, Leather Scraps
